01 · About

Spatial intelligence, grounded in fieldwork

From satellite pixels to survey pillars — I work across the full geospatial pipeline.

I'm a Remote Sensing and GIS Specialist currently serving as Junior Research Fellow (Remote Sensing & GIS) at the Wetland Research and Training Centre, Chilika Development Authority, Odisha — applying GIS and remote sensing to wetland, coastal, and hydrological studies across Chilika Lagoon, Ansupa Lake, and Tampara Lake.

My work integrates satellite data with DGPS (Static & RTK), bathymetric surveys, current-meter surveys, drone imagery, and hydrophone data for dolphin monitoring. I'm a certified Junior Drone Pilot (NSQF Level 3) and have contributed to biodiversity monitoring through 6+ years of annual bird, dolphin, flora, and fauna surveys in Chilika Lagoon.

Earlier, I worked as a DGPS/GIS Technician with the Conservator of Forests, Medininagar Working Plan, Jharkhand, on cadastral georeferencing, forest boundary surveys, and forest cover mapping.
